# ECONOMICS.md - Unit Economics & Lead-Gen Valuation

This document breaks down the unit economics, Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC), Customer Lifetime Value (LTV), and monetization mechanics of **JustCheckin** as a lead magnet for **Credex**.

---

## 1. Lead Value Reasoning
JustCheckin is a free tool. We qualify leads by estimating their SaaS spend and potential savings.
* **Low-Spend Lead (<$100/mo savings):** Small startups or side-projects. Their LTV is low (~$120/yr). We nurture them automatically via email to convert them to Credex self-serve plans.
* **High-Spend Lead (>$500/mo savings):** Teams of 15+ developers overpaying on license tiers. These leads represent immediate consulting opportunities. 
* **The Math:** If a team scales to 30 developers, their annual AI tool spend exceeds $12,000. Through wholesale licensing arbitrage, Credex earns a 20% margin on reselling seats. A single high-spend lead has a contract value of $2,400/yr in recurring arbitrage, and over a 3-year customer lifespan, this yields an **LTV of $7,200**. Therefore, high-spend leads are valued at **$1,500+** based on conversion probabilities and lifetime value.

## 2. Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) by Channel
To scale user acquisition efficiently, we analyze CAC across three primary acquisition channels:
1. **Side-Project Marketing (Product Hunt/Hacker News):**
   * *Projected CAC:* **$0.50 - $1.00**
   * *Details:* Relies on organic distribution. Minor server hosting and maintenance costs ($15/mo) are amortized over high traffic volumes.
2. **Cold Outbound Outreach (Sales Automation):**
   * *Projected CAC:* **$15.00 - $25.00**
   * *Details:* Includes list building tools, LinkedIn Sales Navigator fees, cold mailing software, and manual sales development representative (SDR) labor.
3. **Content Marketing & SEO:**
   * *Projected CAC:* **$5.00 - $8.00**
   * *Details:* Writing detailed pricing comparisons (e.g. Cursor vs Windsurf) and SaaS cost management guides. High up-front writing labor amortized over long-term organic traffic.

## 3. Conversion Funnel Math
Below is the mathematical progression of our lead-generation funnel:

1. **Traffic (Monthly):** 5,000 unique visitors
2. **Audits Run (60%):** 3,000 completed audits
3. **Leads Captured (35%):** 1,050 captured emails (leads)
4. **Qualified High-Savings Leads (15%):** 158 accounts with >$500/mo in savings
5. **Consultation Bookings (25% of qualified):** 40 discovery calls booked
6. **Credex Customer Signups (40% win rate):** 16 new accounts
7. **Average Customer LTV:** $3,600
8. **Total Monthly Lifetime Value Generated:** 16 × $3,600 = **$57,600**
9. **Total Monthly Acquisition Expense:**
   * Organic hosting + API: $15.50
   * Outbound tooling + database credits: $300.00
   * **Total Cost:** $315.50
10. **LTV:CAC Ratio:** $57,600 / $315.50 = **182:1** (Highly efficient outbound funnel).

## 4. Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months
To hit $1M ARR ($83,333 MRR) with an average customer spend generating **$300/mo net MRR** for Credex (about 25 developer seats), we need **278 active enterprise startup accounts**. Below is our quarterly target roadmap:

* **Months 1–3 (Q1): Launch and Proof of Concept**
  * *Target:* 15 active accounts ($4,500 MRR).
  * *Focus:* Launch JustCheckin on Product Hunt and Hacker News. Refine the conversion rate of the email gate.
* **Months 4–6 (Q2): Scale Outbound Engine**
  * *Target:* 45 active accounts ($13,500 MRR).
  * *Focus:* Automate the custom outbound LinkedIn/email campaign using pre-filled audit URLs. Hire 1 offshore SDR.
* **Months 7–9 (Q3): SEO Domination**
  * *Target:* 95 active accounts ($28,500 MRR).
  * *Focus:* Launch dedicated landing pages for every combination of developer tools (e.g. "Cursor vs Copilot calculator").
* **Months 10–12 (Q4): Accelerator Partnerships**
  * *Target:* 160 active accounts ($48,000 MRR).
  * *Focus:* Partner with startup incubators and VC portfolios to offer bulk stack checks to their startup investments.
* **Months 13–18 (Q5–Q6): Platform Expansion & Consolidation**
  * *Target:* 278 active accounts ($83,400 MRR / **$1.008M ARR**).
  * *Focus:* Introduce Brex/Plaid card sync integration to automate audits, expanding target sizes from 10-30 seats to 50-100 seats.
