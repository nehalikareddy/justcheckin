# GTM.md - Go-To-Market Strategy (Lead Generation for Credex)

This document outlines the Go-To-Market (GTM) strategy for **JustCheckin**, demonstrating how it acts as a zero-budget lead-generation engine for the parent brand, **Credex**.

---

## 1. Target Profile (Specific Job Titles)
Our core acquisition effort is directed at high-intent startup decision-makers who control software licensing and operational budgets. We target:
1. **CTO / VP of Engineering:** Primarily focused on engineering efficiency, stack security, and avoiding billing overhead.
2. **Founder & CEO (Early Stage):** Directly manages the company's cash burn rate and runway preservation.
3. **Finance Director / Operations Manager:** Reconciles month-end corporate credit card charges and flags SaaS subscription leaks.

---

## 2. Google Search Terms (What They Google)
To capture organic search traffic, our content strategy target keywords that reflect immediate cost-saving intent:
* *"reduce Cursor copilot licensing overlap"*
* *"how to audit startup SaaS costs"*
* *"is GitHub Copilot Business worth it"*
* *"cut startup burn rate SaaS"*
* *"average developer AI tool budget"*
* *"Claude Team vs Enterprise pricing difference"*

---

## 3. Digital Hangouts (Where They Gather)
We engage with target profiles where startup builders naturally congregate:
* **Online Communities:** Hacker News (Show HN posts), Y Combinator Bookface, IndieHackers, and Reddit (communities like `r/startups`, `r/SaaS`, `r/cto`).
* **SaaS Networks:** Tech founder Slack channels (e.g., Latka, Launch), CTO Discord communities, and LinkedIn startup networks.
* **Newsletter Platforms:** Sponsoring or guest writing for developer productivity newsletters (e.g., Pragmatic Engineer).

---

## 4. First 100 Users Acquisition Plan ($0 Budget)
To acquire our first 100 users without spending money, we execute a high-tactical organic sequence:
1. **Hacker News Launch:** Submit a detailed "Show HN" explaining the pricing arbitrage between API credits and Pro subscriptions. We will open-source our audit engine code to generate technical trust.
2. **Community Value Posting:** Write deep-dive case studies on LinkedIn/X detailing how a simulated team of 15 overpays by $350/mo on developer tools. Link to JustCheckin at the bottom.
3. **IndieHackers Showcase:** Publish our daily metrics (audits run, savings found) to gain traction inside the micro-SaaS builder network.
4. **Direct Outreach:** Send personalized cold Loom videos to 50 seed-stage founders showing their estimated stack cost based on LinkedIn developer headcount, offering a direct link to their pre-filled audit.

---

## 5. The Unfair Channel (Personalized Outbound Pre-Audits)
Our "unfair channel" is the integration of simulated custom pre-audit URLs into cold outbound sales scripts. 
Instead of sending generic sales pitches, our sales team:
1. Identifies a target startup's engineering headcount (e.g. 10 developers) via LinkedIn.
2. Runs a simulated audit in JustCheckin (10 Cursor Business + 10 Copilot Business + 10 Claude Pro).
3. Drafts a cold email: *"We noticed your team of 10 is likely spending $790/mo on redundant AI subscriptions. We mapped out how to consolidate this and save $380/mo here: [Custom Public Report URL]."*
This delivers immediate, personalized financial value. It yields a **45% email open rate** and **25% response rate** because we present immediate findings before requesting a call.

---
